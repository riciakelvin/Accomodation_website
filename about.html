About Everman: Find Rooms and Roommates in a Safe, Verified Community

Everman has established itself as a fast-growing cross-border accommodation platform - providing students with an ideal student housing experience.

With a mindset to streamline and ease the student living experience, finding student accommodation just got easier with Everman

Every year millions of people leave their home country and move to foreign land for work, education and business

All face multiple problems ranging from cultural integration to logistics to regulatory problems

Everman solves house hunting and booking problem for students studying within the state

our approach

Everman is a Long-term accommodation booking platform for students. We wake up every morning to help you find flexible, affordable housing and connect with roommates who make the big city feel like home. Book full-time accommodations near their universities, without the hassle of negotiation, non-standardized and cumbersome paperwork, and broken payment process.
